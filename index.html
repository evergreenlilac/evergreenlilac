About me

Hiya, welcome!

My name is Siret, I am a front-end developer based in Tartu, Estonia. More specifically I am a front-of-the-front-end developer, meaning my expertise is in making web pages beautiful and accessible, but I don't normally deal with implementing business logic. HTML, CSS and presentational JavaScript/TypeScript is where I thrive. If could not write CSS, I would be very sad indeed; I love watching a page or component come together as I build it.

Front-end development isn't all that's in my heart though, there is plenty of room there for all things creative. On this page I have chosen to showcase my art as well as my coding. I sincerely hope that you enjoy your stay.
