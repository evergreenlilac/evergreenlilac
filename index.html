About Me

Hey there, I'm so glad you made it to my page! Welcome!

I'm Siret, front end developer/hobby artist based in Tartu, Estonia.

I learned front end devlopment online and am currently working at Elisa Eesti AS as a junior front end dev. There I help maintain the design system in a future-proof, accessible and responsible way.

As for my artist side, I mainly lean towards digital art with mostly fantastical subject matter, but I dabble in pencil drawing and painting every now and then as well. I consider art to just be a hobby right now, but if you like what I do, I would be more than happy to discuss commission work.

Critique me so that I can improve myself!
