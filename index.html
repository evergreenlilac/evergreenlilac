About Me

Hey there, I'm so glad you made it to my page! Welcome!
I'm Siret, screenname 'Evergreen Lilac'. "Why 'Evergreen Lilac'?" I hear you ask. Well, let me explain. 'Siret' is derived from 'Sirel' which means 'Lilac' in Estonian and 'Evergreen' was inspired by a sight I once encountered in spring: a green lilac bush covered in snow, thriving despite the cold.
I'm an aspiring frontend developer/artist currently residing in Estonia with my significant other and our little rascal Shiba Inu Miko. This page is meant to be a testament to my ever-improving skills in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I really hope you like what you see.
Some trivial information about me:

  • I'm a sucker for time travel sci-fi, my favorite is Doctor Who.
  • I don't smoke or drink coffee, energy drinks or alcohol. Instead, I find relief and strength in the likes of chocolate (nothing above 40% though!) and a nice cup of tea.
  • I'm what some might call a 'casual gamer'. I like to unwind at the end of the day with a few hours of a game I'm obsessed with at that given time.
